1. Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how Starbucks Japan Directory ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you visit our website located at [your-domain.com] (the "Service").

We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about our data practices. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your personal information.

Important Note

We are not affiliated with Starbucks Corporation. This website is an independent editorial project focused on architectural and cultural information about Starbucks locations in Japan.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website, we automatically collect certain information, including:

  • Log Data: IP address, browser type, operating system, referring URLs, and pages viewed
  • Device Information: Device type, screen resolution, and browser settings
  • Usage Data: Time spent on pages, clicks, scrolling behavior, and navigation patterns
  • Location Data: General geographic location based on IP address (country/city level)

2.2 Information You Provide

We may collect information you voluntarily provide when you:

  • Contact us through our contact page or email
  • Submit location suggestions or feedback
  • Report technical issues or errors
  • Subscribe to updates (if available)

2.3 Cookies and Similar Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience and analyze website usage. See our Cookies section for more details.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

3.1 Website Operation and Improvement

  • To provide and maintain our website
  • To analyze usage patterns and improve our content
  • To ensure website security and prevent fraud
  • To optimize website performance and user experience

3.2 Content and Service Enhancement

  • To understand which locations and content are most popular
  • To identify areas for content improvement
  • To develop new features based on user behavior

3.3 Communication

  • To respond to your inquiries and feedback
  • To provide customer support
  • To notify you of important updates or changes

3.4 Advertising

  • To display relevant advertisements through Google AdSense
  • To measure advertising effectiveness
  • To comply with advertising network requirements

4. Third-Party Services

Our website uses several third-party services that may collect information about you:

4.1 Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to analyze website traffic and user behavior. Google Analytics collects information such as:

  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • Geographic location (country/city level)
  • Device and browser information
  • Traffic sources and referral sites

You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on.

4.2 Google AdSense

We display advertisements through Google AdSense, which may use cookies to serve relevant ads based on your browsing behavior. You can control ad personalization through Google Ad Settings.

4.3 Google Maps

Our website may integrate Google Maps for location services. Google's privacy policy applies to their mapping services.

Note: These third-party services have their own privacy policies and data practices. We encourage you to review their privacy policies for more information.

5. Cookies and Tracking

5.1 What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help us remember your preferences and understand how you use our site.

5.2 Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

  • Required for basic website functionality
  • Remember your privacy preferences
  • Enable core features like navigation

Analytics Cookies

  • Help us understand how visitors use our site
  • Provide insights for content improvement
  • Measure website performance

Advertising Cookies

  • Used by Google AdSense to serve relevant ads
  • May track browsing behavior across sites
  • Help measure ad effectiveness

5.3 Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings:

  • Block all cookies (may affect website functionality)
  • Delete existing cookies
  • Set preferences for future cookies
  • Receive notifications when cookies are set

6. Data Protection

6.1 Security Measures

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction:

  • Secure server infrastructure
  • Regular security updates and monitoring
  • Limited access to personal information
  • Secure data transmission protocols

6.2 Data Retention

We retain personal information only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this policy:

  • Analytics data: Up to 26 months (Google Analytics default)
  • Contact information: Until you request deletion
  • Log data: Up to 12 months for security purposes

6.3 International Data Transfers

Some of our third-party service providers may process data outside your country of residence. We ensure these transfers comply with applicable data protection laws.
