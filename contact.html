What You Can Contact Us About

Location Suggestions

Know of a unique Starbucks location we should feature? We're always looking for remarkable spaces that showcase exceptional design or cultural significance.

Please include:

  • Store address and nearest station
  • What makes it architecturally or culturally significant
  • Any historical context you're aware of
  • Photos (if you have permission to share them)

Information Updates

Noticed outdated information or have corrections? We strive for accuracy and appreciate when visitors help us keep our content current.

Let us know about:

  • Store closures or relocations
  • Changes to operating hours
  • New renovations or design updates
  • Factual corrections

Press & Media Inquiries

Media requests, interview opportunities, and collaboration proposals are welcome. Please include details about your publication and specific requirements.

Technical Issues

Experiencing problems with the website or have accessibility concerns? Help us improve by reporting issues.

Please include:

  • Your browser type and version
  • Device type (mobile/desktop)
  • Description of the issue
  • Steps to reproduce the problem

Business Inquiries

For partnership opportunities, advertising inquiries, or business collaborations, please use the email above with "Business Inquiry" in the subject line.

General Feedback

Comments, suggestions for improvement, or just want to share your experience? We love hearing from fellow architecture and design enthusiasts.

Our Response Policy

Response Time: We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24-48 hours during business days. More complex questions may require additional research time.

Priority Responses

We prioritize the following types of inquiries:

  • Urgent corrections to factual information
  • Accessibility issues affecting site usability
  • Business opportunities and partnerships
  • Media requests with deadlines

Response Language

We respond to inquiries in English and Japanese. Please feel free to contact us in whichever language you're most comfortable with.

Other Ways to Connect

While email is our preferred contact method, you can also:

  • Submit location suggestions through our social media channels (when available)
  • Report issues directly if you encounter problems while browsing
  • Share your experiences visiting the locations we've featured

Note: We are not affiliated with Starbucks Corporation. For questions about specific store operations, hours, or menu items, please contact the individual stores directly or visit the official Starbucks Japan website.
